Booking Counselling Appointments at BCIT

All enrolled BCIT students, full-time and part-time, have access to our FREE and CONFIDENTIAL counselling services.

Please call 604-432-8608 to book an appointment with the next available counsellor.

Note: We require at least 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations, otherwise you may be charged a $20.00 Unattended Appointment Fee the first time and $50.00 each time thereafter
